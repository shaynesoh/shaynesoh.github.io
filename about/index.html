About

Me: Shayne Soh

I'm a multidisciplinary designer, illustrator and frontend web developer.

Initially, I intended to be an architect and went to design school fully motivated to become just that. One thing led to another and now I find myself gravitating towards crafting human-centred designs.

What draws me to UX design is how I'm able to iterate creative and practical solutions to meet users' needs and leave a lasting impact on real users. I love to solve problems using human-centred approaches by always putting the user first, and that's why I love UX.

When I'm not working, I'm probably drawing, going on long random walks, or taking photos of my cats.
